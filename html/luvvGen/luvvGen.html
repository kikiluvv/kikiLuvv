luvvIcon v1.0.1

Introducing luvvIcon - the ultimate solution for creating custom favicons that truly reflect your brand. With luvvIcon, designing a unique favicon is no longer reserved for designers with technical skills - anyone can now easily create a favicon that stands out and perfectly complements their website or application.

Our intuitive and user-friendly interface allows you to input various design elements, including the text you want to appear on the favicon, font size, font style, and text color. Choose from an array of background colors, add an icon, and customize it with your preferred icon color. You also have the flexibility to move both the text and the icon around to create the perfect composition that represents your brand.

luvvIcon simplifies the process of creating custom favicons by downloading your design in three different sizes - 16x16, 32x32, and 64x64 - in both PNG and ICO file formats. Your downloaded files are then zipped into a single folder, making it easy to access and use across different platforms.
